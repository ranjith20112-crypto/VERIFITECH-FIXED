FAQs â€” Digital Address Verification

Our AI-powered image recognition technology validates the authenticity of uploaded documents through liveness detection (preventing spoofing), OCR matching of address text, and visual verification of the residence photo against geo-coordinates. We also cross-verify the selfie with the photo ID using facial recognition algorithms to ensure the same person is submitting the verification.

We employ 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit and AES-256 encryption for data at rest. Our systems are ISO/IEC 27001:2013 certified and GDPR compliant. All images are stored in encrypted format with strict access controls, and we conduct regular security audits and penetration testing to ensure data integrity.

Our GPS tracking achieves accuracy within 3-5 meters in urban areas using high-precision satellite triangulation. For rural locations, accuracy ranges between 10-15 meters. We use multiple data points including cell tower triangulation and Wi-Fi positioning to enhance accuracy, along with validation against the declared address in our database.

For digital verification, the turnaround time is 2-5 minutes. For physical verification requirements where a field executive visits the location, the standard TAT is 24-48 hours depending on location tier. Metro cities typically take 24 hours, while Tier 2/3 cities may take up to 48 hours due to travel logistics.

Each verification comes with a unique QR code and digital signature that can be validated through our portal. For physical verifications, we provide geo-tagged photos, timestamp logs, field executive details, and voice recordings of neighbor confirmations where applicable. All data is immutable and stored on secure servers with audit trails.

For employment purposes, we recommend verifying the current residential address where the candidate currently resides. However, for high-security roles or financial services, verifying both current and permanent addresses provides comprehensive risk coverage. Our system allows you to configure which address types are mandatory based on your company policy.
