Yes, our Aadhaar eKYC is fully compliant with UIDAI regulations and the Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA). We use masking protocols to hide the first 8 digits of Aadhaar numbers as per RBI guidelines, storing only the last 4 digits for reference. Our system is certified by UIDAI as a KYC User Agency (KUA).

Our AI engine runs multiple checks: (1) Image forensics to detect Photoshop or editing artifacts, (2) Comparison of document security features (holograms, watermarks, fonts), (3) Cross-validation with government databases in real-time, and (4) Liveness detection during selfie capture to ensure the person is physically present and not using a photo or video replay.

Our OCR technology achieves 99.5% accuracy for clear, legible documents. For Aadhaar and PAN cards specifically, accuracy exceeds 99.8% due to standardized formats. In cases of poor image quality or glare, our system prompts the user to retake the photo immediately, ensuring only high-quality data enters the verification pipeline.

All PII (Personally Identifiable Information) is encrypted using AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. We are ISO 27001:2013 certified and GDPR compliant. Data retention policies auto-delete verification logs after 7 years as per legal requirements. Access is restricted via role-based controls (RBAC) and multi-factor authentication (MFA).

Our system has built-in retry logic with exponential backoff for temporary UIDAI/NSDL outages. If services remain unavailable, we queue the request and notify you once verification completes (typically within minutes). For critical time-sensitive cases, we offer offline verification modes using our cached reference database with manual validation protocols.
