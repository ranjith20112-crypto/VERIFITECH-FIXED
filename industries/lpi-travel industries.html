Industry Challenges

Why Hospitality Needs Workforce Verification

Guest Safety & Security

Hotel staff and housekeepers access private guest rooms and handle valuable assets. Verify criminal history, past employment, and trustworthiness to prevent theft and ensure guest security.

High Turnover Rates

With 74% turnover in hospitality vs 15% in other industries, rapid yet thorough screening is essential. Pre-empt verification for seasonal hiring and bulk recruitment without compromising safety.

Vendor & Supplier Risk

Food suppliers, transport vendors, and maintenance contractors enter your premises daily. Screen third-party vendors, delivery personnel, and outsourced staff to maintain property security standards.
