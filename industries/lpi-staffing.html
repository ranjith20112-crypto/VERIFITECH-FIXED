Industry Challenges

Why Recruiters Need Fast Verification

Time-to-Hire Pressure

Clients demand quick placements. Delayed background checks cost placements. Need 24-48 hour turnaround for criminal checks, employment verification, and reference checks without sacrificing accuracy.

Candidate Fraud Risk

30% of resumes contain false information. Fake degrees, inflated titles, and hidden criminal histories expose clients to risk. Need robust identity verification and credential authentication.

Volume & Scale Issues

Seasonal hiring spikes and mass recruitment drives require processing hundreds of checks simultaneously. Manual processes create bottlenecks. Need automated bulk screening solutions.
